Mera is the wife of Aquaman, the Queen of Atlantis and a member of the Aquaman Family known for her strength and passion. They had a son together named Arthur Curry, Jr. although he was killed by Black Manta. Originally she is from a place called Xebel in the otherworldly Dimension Aqua, and has a twin sister named Hila. She has also been a member of the Red Lantern Corps and the Justice League. Mera was created by Jack Miller and Nick Cardy, first appearing in Aquaman #11. (1963)
